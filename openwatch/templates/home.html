welcome to openwatch!

 

Welcome to OpenWatch, a participatory counter-surveillance project. OpenWatch is a global project which uses cellular phones as a way of monitoring authority figures.

OpenWatch.net is the web counterpart to the Cop Recorder and OpenWatch Recorder applications for Android and iPhone. The apps, which secretly record media, then anonymously upload it are available for download here.

To browse all of the recordings submitted by OpenWatch contributors, click here to see the Media gallery.

To read more about the OpenWatch philosophy, technology and ways you can get involved, visit the about page.
