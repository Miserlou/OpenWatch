welcome to openwatch!

 

Welcome to OpenWatch, a project to create and store citizen-produced recordings of power use and abuse from around the world!

OpenWatch.net is the web counterpart to the Cop Recorder and OpenWatch Recorder applications for Android. The apps are available for download here.

To browse all of the recordings submitted by OpenWatch contributors, click here to see the Media gallery.

To read more about the OpenWatch philosophy, technology and ways you can get involved, visit the about page.
