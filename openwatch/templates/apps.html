Apps

 

The OpenWatch project provides Android and iOS applications for recording media, OpenWatch Recorder and Cop Recorder 2. Both are capable of secretly recording media and uploading it to the OpenWatch server.

Android

OpenWatch recorder can secretly record audio and video, and upload it to the OpenWatch server along with geolocation data. It is available on the Android Market for 2.0 and greater devices.


Cop Recorder is currently available on the Android Market for Android 1.5 and above devices. It can secretly record audio and then upload it to the OpenWatch server along with location data.


iPhone

Cop Recorder is currently available on the Apple App Store for iOS 3.0 and above devices. It can secretly record audio and then upload it to the OpenWatch server along with location data.
